A Brief History of Cricket

Cricket is one of the oldest and most popular sports in the world.
The game originated in England in the 16th century and has since spread globally, with passionate fans in countries like India, Australia, and South Africa.

Why Cricket is Loved

Cricket is a strategic and skillful game. It can be played anywhere, from grassy fields to streets, making it accessible to people of all ages and backgrounds.
It brings together communities and inspires fierce competition between nations.
